One way to easily understand architecture is to start with the basics and build upon that knowledge. Here are a few steps you can take to get a better understanding of architectural features.

different architectural styles

There are many different architectural styles throughout history, such as Gothic, Baroque, Art Deco, etc. Each style has its own characteristics and features that can be easily recognized.

about the elements of architectur

Every building has certain basic elements, such as walls, roofs, and doors. Understanding how these elements are used and combined can help you understand the overall design of a building.

Visit architectural landmarks

Take a tour of famous architectural landmarks in your area, or visit a virtual tour online. This can help you see the different styles and elements of architecture in action and understand how they are used to create different effects.

Architecture related books

Read architecture related books, blogs or watch documentaries: There are many books, blogs, and documentaries available that can provide you with more in-depth information about architecture and its history.
